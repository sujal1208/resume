Intro

  • Ah, so you've landed on my personal page—whether by accident or by design, I’m just glad you're here.
  • After much deliberation, I finally decided to give my website a makeover. Sure, I could have spent those hours on something more *useful* (like learning how to bake sourdough bread), but instead, I chose to make this little corner of the internet a bit more *me*.
  • Now, you might be asking yourself, “Who exactly am I reading about?” Well, here’s the good news: I’m a human. You can breathe easy now. But more specifically, I’m Sujal Patel, the creator, curator, and occasional comedian behind this site.
  • You might be wondering why I decided to create this page in the first place. After all, I’ve had a personal website for a while—mainly as a digital version of my resume. But, as I’ve learned over the years, there’s so much more to a person than a list of job titles and fancy bullet points.
  • Since graduating, I’ve learned a ton in my career—lessons I’d love to share with anyone stepping into the world of IT. This website is my way of giving back, offering insights, advice, and maybe a little bit of inspiration for the next generation of tech enthusiasts. And, of course, it’s also a space where you can get to know *me*—not just as a professional, but as a person.
  • So whether you're here to learn, laugh, or just satisfy your curiosity, welcome. You've found your way to my corner of the web, and I’m excited to have you.
